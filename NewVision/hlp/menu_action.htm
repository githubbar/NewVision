Action menu commands

The Action menu offers the following commands:

Play

Starts tracking process.

Pause

Pauses tracking process. Clicking "Play" again will resume from the current frame.

Stop

Stops tracking process, rewinds to the beginning of the sequence (or to offset specified), resets all tracking data structures.

Record

If you are using a video file as your input, you can specify to start tracking from any frame in the file. Typical videos have a framerate of 30 FPS so, for example, to start tracking 30 seconds after the sequence begins type in 900 (30*30=900).

Left click

In this sub-menu you can choose how left mouse click works in the main document area.
