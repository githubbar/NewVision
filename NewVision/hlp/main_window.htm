New Vision™ Main Window

New Vision™ main window consists of 4 tiled information output panels. Panels display various types of tracking information. You can choos what information to display through the Display menu. Display menu can be also invoked by right clicking at the correspoinding view.

Left panel

Always displays video from the file or camera; additionaly you can supersimpose various tracking information.

Right panel

Blank by default; here you can display various tracking information.

Floor panel

Displays tracking information in "floor view" (i.e. view from the top)

Extra panel

Not implemented.
