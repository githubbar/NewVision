Display menu commands

New Vision™ main window consists of 4 tiled information output panels. This menu controls what information is displayed at four output panels.

Left

  • Bodies - Draws ellipsoids, representing tracking bodies, projected onto the image surface each with a unique ID on top.
  • Blobs - Draws blobs with marked head/foot candidates.
  • Groups - Encloses groups of people into a white rectangle.
  • Sales Reps - Marks sales representative with an "S"
  • Dwelling Bodies - Marks dwelling people with "?"
  • Time Stamp - Puts a timestamp in the lower right corner.

Right

  • Backgound - Shows a visual representation of the current background.
  • Bodies - Draws ellipsoids, representing tracking bodies, projected onto the image surface each with a unique ID on top.
  • Blobs - Draws blobs with marked head/foot candidates.
  • Groups - Encloses groups of people into a white rectangle.
  • Paths - Draws paths travelled by tracked people.
  • Doors - Display the doors.
  • Camera - Display the location of the camera (might outside of the image)

Floor

  • Floormap - Displays a floor model (either image or CAD)
  • Body maps - Displays body locations as dots on the floor
  • Body paths - Displays body paths (with circles at the dwell locations)
  • Sales Reps - Displays sales people.
  • Heat Map - Draws a heat map of where people spend most of the time.
  • Obstacles - Displays obstacles.
  • Time Stamp - Puts a timestamp in the lower right corner.

Extra

Not implemented.
